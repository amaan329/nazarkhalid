Nazar Khalid

PhD Student, Demography & Population Studies

Population Studies Center & Department of Sociology, University of Pennsylvania

Nazar grew up in Bihar and Maharashtra (India). He completed his BA in Economics at University of Delhi and MA/MPhil in Economics at Jawaharlal Nehru University. He is currently pursuing a joint PhD in Demography & Sociology at Penn and is the recipient of the Presidential PhD Fellowship.

His research spans climatic disruption, education & health inequality, social stratification, and survey & mixed methods approaches — especially in South Asia. With r.i.c.e., he has led phone surveys on discrimination and rural panel surveys on sanitation and clean fuel use.
