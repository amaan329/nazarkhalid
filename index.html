About

Nazar Khalid is a researcher at the University of Pennsylvania whose work focuses on family demography, fertility, and social change. His research explores how demographic transitions influence family structures, intergenerational support, and social policy in South Asia and beyond.

Research Interests

  • Family demography and intergenerational relationships
  • Fertility transitions and family planning in South Asia
  • Education and child well-being
  • Health, aging, and caregiving dynamics
  • Population change and social policy

Research

Nazar's research examines demographic processes, fertility decisions, and the consequences of population change. His work combines quantitative and qualitative methods to understand family, health, and education outcomes.
