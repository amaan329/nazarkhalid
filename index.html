Nazar Khalid

PhD Student, Demography & Sociology (Joint)

Population Studies Center & Department of Sociology, University of Pennsylvania

Presidential PhD Fellow at Penn. Previously BA (Economics) at University of Delhi; MA & MPhil (Economics) at Jawaharlal Nehru University. Grew up in Bihar and Maharashtra, India.

Research: climatic disruptive events; education & health inequalities; social stratification; early childhood development; marginalized youth; quantitative, survey, & mixed methods; evaluation and policy analysis; South Asia.
